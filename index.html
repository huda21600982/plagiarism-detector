How it works under the hood

Embeddings, FAISS & translation

Your text is translated to English (if needed), split into chunks, converted into multilingual sentence embeddings, and compared against your corpus using a FAISS index with cosine similarity.

Local-first design

Corpus files and indexes live on your machine or server. Ideal for university projects, offline demos, or privacy-focused setups.
